Barcelona was a great ecosystem to participate in dissemination activities. I enjoy bringing research to general society. Together with with Barcelona Activa and #100tífiques I have presented in several occasions my doctoral thesis. I have been a mentor at Inspira STEAM programme. Their aim is to spark the curiosity of children in science, make children aware about gender stereotypes, and empower girls into pursuing STEAM careers. Always happy to take part in initiatives for bridging the gender gap, increasing diversity and bringing closer a more egalitarian society. Do not hesitate to contact me if this is your case and you think I could be of help.
